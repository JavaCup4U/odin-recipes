Crispy Baked Chicken Legs

Description

This recipe is one that many who are watching what they eat on a daily basis or just need protein in their diet and less carbohydrates. Every serving will yield you about 300 calories and only 2 grams of carbohydrates. Which is great for someone who has a strict goal of cutting down on too many heavy carbohydrates.

Or it is for anyone really who just enjoys crispy chicken, I know I do. It's an easy and quick meal for 8 people. Only takes a total of 50 minutes tops for cook time. Definitely a recipe worth giving a shot!

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds chicken legs
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons tamari

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Step 2: Place chicken legs in a large bowl. Spread chili powder, garlic powder, cayenne, and salt onto the chicken; pour sauce over top. Mix thoroughly and transfer to a baking sheet.
  3. Step 3: Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, 45 to 50 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
