Strawberry Pie Recipe

Description

This recipe makes 8 servings which is more than enough for everyone to enjoy! Only 265 calories per serving and 41 grams of carbohydrates.This is for those who enjoy a nice dessert after lunch or dinner every once in a while. This strawberry pie can be ready to go in less than an hour.

The recipe only calls for strawberries, pie crust, white sugar, cornstarch, water, and whipping cream. That's it! Just those 6 ingredients to get started with this delicious treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 1 quart fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¾ cup water
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Step 1: Arrange half of strawberries in baked pastry shell. Mash remaining berries and combine with sugar in a medium saucepan. Place saucepan over medium heat and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.
  2. Step 2: In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water. Gradually stir cornstarch mixture into boiling strawberry mixture. Reduce heat and simmer mixture until thickened, about 10 minutes, stirring constantly. Pour mixture over berries in pastry shell.
  3. Step 3: Chill for several hours before serving. In a small bowl, whip cream until soft peaks form. Serve each slice of pie with a dollop of whipped cream.
